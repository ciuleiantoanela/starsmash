Met Gala RANKED
by Color Theory

We did the math. The results r SHOCKING. 😱🎨

By Ana-Antoanela Ciulei · Fashion Forensics · May 2026

Listen up hunny — every yr the stars descend on the steps in outfits curated by a TEAM of professionals. So WHY does it still look like a color wheel exploded? STAR SMASH! consulted actual color theory (we read a BOOK 📚) and ranked the looks from CATASTROPHE to chef's KISS. 💋

#6 💀

TIFFANEE SPARX in "Neon Trauma"

Color theory: All four primary/secondary at full saturation. No dominant hue. No harmony. Just vibes — and the vibes r SCREAMING. 🚨

Score: 2/10 — call her stylist's MOTHER 📞

#5 😬

CRYSTAL LAMOURE in "Bridesmaid From Hell"

Color theory: Pastel ANALOGOUS palette but muddled values. Everything reads the SAME brightness — flat, lifeless, somehow both bridal AND nauseating. 🤢

Score: 4/10 — try a focal point next time 🎯

#4 😐

BRENDYN CASHE in "Beige Mid-Life Crisis"

Color theory: Textbook MONOCHROMATIC. Safe. Refined. Boring as HELL. Sources say he asked his stylist for "quiet luxury." Mission accomplished — we forgot abt him by the next photo. 💤

Score: 5/10 — fine, but at what cost

#3 💅

MOXII MOON in "Acid Sunset"

Color theory: ANALOGOUS with a dramatic tonal contrast — the deep purple anchors the warm pink/orange and stops it from feeling cheap. CHEFS KISS. 💋

Score: 8/10 — main character behavior ⭐

#2 🔥

THE TWINZ in "Coordinated Warfare"

Color theory: SPLIT COMPLEMENTARY (pink + teal). High contrast. The white grounds it. Heiress sisters in PERFECT harmony — even tho they HATE each other. Iconic. 💖

Score: 9/10 — DRAG ur enemies in style

#1 👑

ANONYMOUS NEWCOMER in "Triadic Witchcraft"

Color theory: TRIADIC palette — three colors evenly spaced on the color wheel. Hardest combo to pull off, she made it look EASY. Bold but balanced. A FORCE. Sources say she's never been to a Met Gala before. Who IS she?? 👀

Score: 10/10 — A STAR IS BORN ⭐

📚 COLOR THEORY DECODED

  • 🎨 Monochromatic: 1 color, diff shades
  • 🌈 Analogous: 3 neighbors on the wheel
  • ⚡ Complementary: opposites attract
  • 🔥 Split-complementary: one + two neighbors of its opposite
  • ✨ Triadic: 3 colors evenly spaced
